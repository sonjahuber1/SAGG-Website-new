Our Founders

Founded by Annetta Paps-King and Lisa Zscheye, SaGG Foundation champions girls' education in The Gambia. Our founders combine their expertise in education and community development to create lasting change.

"If girls have so much to offer to the world, why not invest in their power"

- Annetta Paps-King

"I always question, why the disparity in opportunities for the female gender"

- Lisa Zscheye

Our Vision

A Gambia where every girl has access to quality education, empowering them to break the cycle of poverty and create lasting change in their communities.

Our Mission

  • Sponsor girls' education in The Gambia
  • Ensure 100% of donations reach students
  • Partner with local communities
  • Create sustainable educational programs

The SaGG Foundation (Sponsor a Gambian Girl) is a girl's education movement, with aim of championing the cause for girl child education in The Gambia. Education is a basic human right; our vision is to advocate and champion for female education.
